Overview

Purpose

Market services effectively, interact with customers, speed up bookings, build credibility and remain competitive in the digital age. It also provides opportunities for data-driven improvements and greater adaptability in the face of unexpected challenges.

Audience

We cater to adventure enthusiasts and thrill-seekers of diverse backgrounds and interests. Our target customers include:

Age Group: Primarily individuals aged 18 to 45, including young adults, professionals, and active retirees.
Financial Situation: Middle to upper-middle income, with disposable income for recreational activities.
Interests: Outdoor adventure, adrenaline rushes, exploring nature, and seeking unique and memorable experiences.

What They Want:
Our target audience seeks heart-pounding excitement and memorable adventures. They're looking for experiences that offer a break from routine and the chance to connect with nature and like-minded thrill-seekers.

What They Expect to Find:
When accessing our website, our audience expects to find:
Trip Information: Details about our rafting trips, including difficulty levels, locations, and durations.
Safety and Professionalism: Information on safety measures, guides' expertise, and quality equipment.
Booking Convenience: Easy-to-use booking and reservation options, with transparent pricing.
Customer Reviews: Reviews and testimonials from previous adventurers to build trust.
Adventure Content: Inspiring stories, photos, and videos showcasing the excitement of our trips.

Unmet Needs:
While our target audience is primarily interested in thrilling rafting experiences, some unmet needs may include:
Customization: More personalized trip options to cater to varying levels of experience.
Information Accessibility: Accessibility features for differently-abled adventure enthusiasts.
Multi-Device Access: Ensuring a responsive design for mobile devices, laptops, and desktops for easy access and booking.

How They Access the Website:
Our audience is likely to access our website through a variety of devices, including mobile phones for quick browsing and reservations, laptops for more detailed planning, and desktops for in-depth research and reviews. Therefore, it's essential to ensure a responsive and user-friendly design across all platforms to meet their needs effectively.
